About Us

Who We Are

Founded in 2022, Ambi-Mining is dedicated to responsible mining practices that prioritize sustainability and social responsibility. We extract natural resources such as gold, lithium, and other minerals in Africa and beyond to supply the needs of a rapidly growing international market.

Our balanced portfolio of mining assets allows us to process ores and supply customers around the world. We believe in contributing to sustainable development by investing in social initiatives and minimizing our environmental footprint.

Mission
To extract natural resources in a responsible and sustainable manner, while creating value for our customers, employees, and local communities.
Vision
To become a leading mining company that drives sustainable economic development across Africa and beyond.
